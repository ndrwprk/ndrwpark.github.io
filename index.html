As this is my first web-based chef-d'oeuvre, I decided
to make it a digital personification of myself.

This cocktail I call my 'professional website' is inspired by my free spirit,
three-piecer ambition and my passion for art & programming. Quite the charming
mix of minimalism, responsive web design, and HTML5/CSS3/skel. Cheers!

My top 5 strengths:

  • 1. Command: has presence, can take control of a situation, make decisions.
  • 2. Ideation: fascinated by ideas and able to find connections between seemingly disparate phenomena.
  • 3. Activator: turns thoughts into action by identifying a goal, devise numerous ways of reaching it, then choosing the best alternative.

Art & Music

Since childhood, I have been trained in the fine arts and concert orchestra (piano, clarinet, & bassoon). So, I've always had deep appreciation for art and music. To this day, I draw and paint as well as DJ in my free time. Some of my art can be found here.

Organizations

I am a member of USACS (Undergraduate Student Alliance of Computer Scientists) where I help educate students as well as participate and volunteer at hackRU. I am also a member of RU SHRM (Rutgers University Chapter of Human Resource Management).

Social
