  • Who Am I?

Hey, I'm Andrew!

I'm a broke college student with big dreams. I may be young, but I pride myself on my versatility as a professional and a free spirit. My friends describe me as ambitious, charismatic, clever, and fun. Also, I excel behind a keyboard, canvas, and speakers and have a knack for creativity and ideas.

I love loose leaf tea, my neurotic cat has four different names, not a huge fan of IPAs, I watch animated movies like it's my life, I've backpacked a foreign country alone at the age of 16, I swim at least once a week and I ate a scorpion before.

Recently, I have sparked an interest in web design and development mainly because my background in art complements the field well. However, I am also fascinated by other areas in computer science such as software engineering, algorithms & data structures, and computer security.
